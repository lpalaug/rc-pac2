Active FSCA Manufacturerback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Manufacturers linked to devices with incidents that have open FSCAs are classified as active FSCA manufacturers.

Medical Device c(?d) , is manufactured by op(?d,?m) , has incident op(?d,?i) , has field safety corrective action op(?i,?f) , has status op(?f,?Open) -> Active FSCA Manufacturer c(?m)

Critical Incidentback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Incidents linked to risks with critical severity are classified as critical incidents.

Incident c(?i) , has risk op(?i,?r) , has severity op(?r,?Critical) -> Critical Incident c(?i)

High Risk Device ClassDback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Devices classified under risk class D are automatically considered high-risk devices.

Medical Device c(?d) , has risk class op(?d,?ClassD) -> High Risk Device c(?d)

High Risk Device ClassIIIback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Devices classified under risk class III are automatically considered high-risk devices.

Medical Device c(?d) , has risk class op(?d,?ClassIII) -> High Risk Device c(?d)

Unresolved Incidentback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Serious incidents without corrective actions taken are classified as unresolved incidents.

Incident c(?i) , has incident type op(?i,?SeriousIncident) , corrective action taken dp(?i,"false"^^boolean) -> Unresolved Incident c(?i)
